# Smart Sprinkler Control

Intelligent, weather-aware irrigation management for Home Assistant. Built on a
Zero Sensor Pollution architecture — a single rich summary sensor exposes all
zone, schedule, and weather data instead of flooding your entity registry.

## Key Features

- **Zone control** — Start, stop, and configure up to 32 watering zones with
  per-zone duration, flow rate, and water-usage tracking.
- **Scheduling** — Flexible multi-zone schedules by time and day of week, with
  per-zone durations and automatic execution.
- **Weather-based rain delay** — Automatically skips or shortens watering based
  on current conditions, recent rainfall, and forecast precipitation.
- **Precipitation widget** — At-a-glance rain/snow status and accumulation
  surfaced directly in the dashboard.
